About me

The website creator

Hey, it's me, the creator, my online alias is AngelFluffyOokami, I'm a 17 year old hobbyist photographer, how is it that I became a photographer? Well, let's go back in time, back to 2016, I'm a 13 year old teen in middle school, I had an interest in photography since I can remember, but one day, my art teacher bought his DSLR to school

I then asked my teacher if I could use it, and he let me use it, so I was going All around school, using the camera, and that's when I found out I had a love for photography, fast forward, 2018, I get my first camera, a Sony A37, I fell in love with it, it was a pretty awesome camera, then fast forward, it's February 2020, I get a new idea, to start a photography collective, which is why we're here, on Our Collective, this went live on March 6th 2020.
